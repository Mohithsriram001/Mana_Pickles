About Mana Pickles

Mana Pickles is a home-grown brand from Telangana, bringing you authentic, traditional Veg & Non-veg pickles made with pure ingredients, clean preparation, and the same love as home food. Every batch is prepared in small quantities to maintain freshness, hygiene, and real Telangana flavor.

  • ✅ 100% Homemade
  • ✅ No Preservatives or Artificial Colors
  • ✅ Prepared using Country Chicken, Fresh Mutton, Fish & Prawns
  • ✅ Packed in Food-grade Containers
  • ✅ Delivered Across India
